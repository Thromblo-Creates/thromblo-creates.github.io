Sage & Pestle is a cozy idle gardening and crafting game played inside Discord.
Players plant seeds, care for gardens, collect items, improve a facility, process
harvests into better goods, and discover new strains through breeding.

## Highlights

- **Plant and tend** living gardens that grow over time.
- **Process harvests** into more valuable crafted goods.
- **Breed new strains** to discover and collect.
- **Upgrade your facility** to expand what you can grow and make.
- **Profile customization** and player data tools, with a clear privacy policy and terms.

## Tech

Built with TypeScript, discord.js Components V2, and Prisma against PostgreSQL.
The whole bot runs locally for development against a local database, with seed data
and migrations to get a fresh environment going quickly.
